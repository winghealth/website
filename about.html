About us

Mission

Wing exists to increase equitable access to virtual health care in a COVID-19 world

Our Story

The team met at the MIT COVID-19 Challenge - Beat The Pandemic and coalesced around the idea of hyperlocal telehealth delivery within their local communities. We were selected as a top team for the track focusing on new ways to deliver care in a COVID-19 world. Wing has also participated in the Sling Health Bootcamp and UCSF COVID-19 Hackathon.
